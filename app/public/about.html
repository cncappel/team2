About Us

Who we are:

Indiana Soccer Referees Association is responsible for assigning referees to U.S.S.F.-sanctioned club matches in the greater-Indianapolis area. Our goal is to create one central place where you can find all your information for anything you need. We aim to create and grow a qualified group of referees to help our Indiana soccer games run smoothly. Referees are held to the highest standards as they are responsible for maintaining a safe and enjoyable playing environment for Indiana Soccer Association members of all ages and skill levels.

Our vision:

To help referees have on central place to find their games and their performance information.

Our mission:

To empower soccer officials with the necessary tools to officiate the game of soccer in a timely and professional manner.
